Biography

Woody Tai is an emerging scientist from Hong Kong, dedicated to bringing the traditional poles of knowlwedge together — the sciences and humanities. With a strong foundation in Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science, Woody holds the very spiritis where science was founded — curiosity. Woody has dedicated his teen years to make the voices of youth heard, from the first TEDx event in Hong Kong led by a non-international student body to taking up the foundations of research that ultimatly led him to win Top Awards at the International Science and Enginnering Fair (2025) for his work in making the world greener.
